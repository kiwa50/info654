Paper making DIY

Making paper was a fun, but laborious activity. I wrote ALOT of letters to friends during the quarantine. My friend, Nina has a typewriter and I thought it would be a fun project to create paper that I could send to her, and have her send me it back with a letter she had typewritten on.

You will need the following ingredients: 1 1/2 cup and 3 tablespoons of all purpose flour
